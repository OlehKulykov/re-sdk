Enumeration Type Documentation

enum _reTextureFilterType

Structure used for typing of texture filters.

Enumerator
RETextureFilterNone 

No texture filter.

RETextureFilterNearest 

Nearest texture filter. On OpenGL/ES textures settes 'GL_TEXTURE_MIN_FILTER' to 'GL_NEAREST' and 'GL_TEXTURE_MAG_FILTER' to 'GL_NEAREST'.

RETextureFilterLinear 

Linear texture filter. On OpenGL/ES textures settes 'GL_TEXTURE_MIN_FILTER' to 'GL_LINEAR' and 'GL_TEXTURE_MAG_FILTER' to 'GL_LINEAR'.

RETextureFilterMipmapedStandart 

Mipmaped standart linear texture filter. On OpenGL/ES textures settes 'GL_TEXTURE_MIN_FILTER' to 'GL_LINEAR_MIPMAP_NEAREST' and 'GL_TEXTURE_MAG_FILTER' to 'GL_LINEAR'.

RETextureFilterMipmapedTrilinear 

Mipmaped trilinear texture filter. On OpenGL/ES textures settes 'GL_TEXTURE_MIN_FILTER' to 'GL_LINEAR_MIPMAP_LINEAR' and 'GL_TEXTURE_MAG_FILTER' to 'GL_LINEAR'.
